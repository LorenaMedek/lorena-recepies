Zebra-pie

You want to make something impressiv,but baking is not your speciality. Don't worry! This cake is very easy and looks amazing!

PREP TIME 30 MIN
BAKING TIME 35-40 MIN
SERVINGS 12

Ingredients

  • 300g flour
  • 1 package baking powder
  • 200 g sugar
  • 4 Eggs
  • 250ml oil
  • 100ml milk
  • 2 tbsp baking cocoa
  • 1 package vanilla sugar
  • 100 g powdered sugar
  • 2 - 3 tbsp lemon juice

STEP 1

Preheat the oven to 180 degrees (fan oven: 160 degrees). Line a springform pan (Ã˜ 26 cm) with baking paper. Mix flour with baking powder and sugar in a bowl. Add eggs, oil and milk and beat until frothy for 2 minutes. Pour half of the dough into a bowl and mix with cocoa and vanilla sugar.

STEP 2

Now put 2-3 tablespoons of the light dough in the middle of the springform pan base. Place 2-3 tablespoons of dark dough directly in the middle of the light dough. This causes the dough to begin to flow outwards. Always use the two doughs in this way, starting from the middle, until the base of the springform pan is completely covered. Bake in the oven for approx. 35 minutes. Turn the oven to fan for the next 3-5 minutes for a crispy edge. Let the cake cool out.

STEP 3

Remove the zebra cake from the mold and place on a cake plate. Mix the powdered sugar with lemon juice and spread it thinly over the cake so that the marbling can still be seen from above.

Bon appetit!

Muffins with chocolate and cream

You want to eat something fluffy and sweet,but you don't have much time,then stop here and take a look. This recepie is exactly what you need. This muffins are easy to make and taste fantastic!

PREP TIME 20 MIN
BAKING TIME 25 MIN
SERVINGS 12

Ingredients for 12 muffins...

  • 300 g flour
  • 2 TL backing powder
  • 110 g brown sugar
  • 1 package of vanilla sugar
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 3 eggs
  • 150 ml oil
  • 250 g cream
  • 150 g dark chocolate chunks

STEP 1

Line muffin tin (12 wells) with paper cases. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees (fan oven: 160 degrees).

STEP 2

Mix flour with baking powder in a bowl. Add sugar, vanilla sugar and salt. Add eggs, oil and cream and stir the ingredients together. If necessary, chop the chocolate roughly. Set aside 2-3 tablespoons of the chopped chocolate.

STEP 3

Stir the remaining chocolate into the batter and distribute evenly among the 12 muffin cavities. Sprinkle the reserved chocolate onto the muffins and bake in the preheated oven for approx. 25 minutes. Turn the oven to fan for the next 2-3 minutes for a crispy edge. Let the Muffins cool out a bit and the enjoy!

Bon appetit!

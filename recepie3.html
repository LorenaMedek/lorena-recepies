American Cookies

These cookies are the best! They have a crispy edge,but are very soft inside. After you try this recepie you will never go buy cookies at the store anymore.

PREP TIME 25 MIN
BAKING TIME 12 MIN
SERVINGS 15-20

Ingredients for 15-20 cookies...

  • 250 g soft butter
  • 100 g sugar
  • 150g brown sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs (size M)
  • 360g flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 200 g chocolate chunks

STEP 1

Beat soft butter with the two types of sugar for several minutes until creamy. Add vanilla extract and eggs and mix briefly. Mix the flour, baking powder and salt, add to the egg-butter mixture and stir into a dough.

STEP 2

Fold approx. 180 g of the chocolate chunks into the dough using a wooden spoon or spatula. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees top/bottom heat (fan oven: 160 degrees). Line your baking trays with baking paper.

STEP 3

Form the dough into equal-sized balls with your hands and place them on the tray with plenty of space between them. The dough makes about 15 large cookies or 20 smaller ones, depending on your preference.

STEP 4

Distribute the remaining chocolate chunks over the dough balls and press them down lightly. Bake the cookies in the preheated oven for approx. 10 minutes. Turn the oven for fan for the netx 2 minutes. The edge should not become too dark, the middle of the biscuit should still look a little moist. Then bake the second tray. Let the cookies cool for about 10 minutes. As they cool, the cookies will become a little firmer.

Bon appetit!
